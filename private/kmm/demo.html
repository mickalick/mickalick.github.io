About Us

KM Makes exists for one reason: to help humans serve other humans better.

No matter the size or goals of your organization (or lack thereof), there can be endless questions in your mind about how you can best deliver value to both you and your audience, and what paths you can take to get there.

That's where we come in. KM Makes is a full spectrum design service, helping organizations from as small as one person to large-scale corporations realize the potential of their products through a human-centered approach.

Services

Our services range from high-level product strategy, all the way to delivering research-backed, developer hand-off ready mockups and prototypes. We meet you wherever you're at in your lifecycle, understand your goals, and go from there.

Pricing

The first hour is always on the house, with options after ranging from hourly rates to project-sized pricing. We understand budgets are always tight, so we'll work with you to find something that works for all involved.
