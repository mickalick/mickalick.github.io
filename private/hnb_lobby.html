Huntington Digital Lobby

Project Overview

In my time working at Huntington National Bank, I was fully dedicated to the conception and launch of a new to bank application that supports the sales and onboarding of business and commercial customers, including prospective customers, to the bank.

As the lead interaction designer on the project, I was responsible for collaborating across multiple areas of the business, between product partners, process SMEs, architecture, and various technical teams, to design a viable solution that would meet the needs of the business and its customers alike. Here's how I did it.
