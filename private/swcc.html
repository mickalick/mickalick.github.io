Sherwin-Williams Collision Core

From September 2019 to October 2020, I worked in a contractor role with Sherwin-Williams where I was fully dedicated to the conception and launch of a new suite of B2B applications supporting their automotive business customers.

As a UX designer on the project, I assisted with user research efforts, contributed to product strategy and roadmaps, collaborated with external vendors, and lead front-end design efforts on multiple applications.
