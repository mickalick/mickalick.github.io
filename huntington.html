Huntington National Bank

Starting in March 2021, I have worked as a mid-level Interaction Designer for the User Experience team at Huntington National Bank. In my time at Huntington, I have been fully dedicated to the conception and launch of a new to bank application that supports the sales and onboarding of business and commercial banking customers, including prospective customers. As the lead UX resource on the project, I am responsible for managing the deliverables needed across interaction, visual, and content design areas, in collaboration with multiple Agile development teams. I am also primarily responsible for interaction design artifacts, including flows, information archictecture, and wireframes, both high-level to fully annotated specifications.

Due to the confidential nature of this project, I'm unable to display any of my work online. If you'd like to learn more about my work at Huntington, please contact me.
