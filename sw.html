Sherwin-Williams

From September 2019 to October 2020, I worked in a contractor role with Sherwin-Williams, where I was fully dedicated to the conception and launch of a brand new B2B application suite. These applications will support their automotive business customers, offering solutions that simplify existing repair processes, while generating data to identify and predict work chain inefficiencies. My work on the project has involved a wide-range of UX activities, from discovery research and persona work, to application design and release testing.

Due to the confidential nature of this project, I'm unable to display any of my work online. If you'd like to learn more about my work with Sherwin-Williams, please contact me.
