About

KM Makes exists for one reason: to help humans serve other humans better.

No matter the size or goals of your organization (or lack thereof), there can be endless questions in your mind about how you can best deliver value to both you and your audience, and what paths you can take to get there.

That's where we come in. KM Makes is a digital design service that helps organizations from as small as one person to large-scale corporations realize the potential of their products through a human-centered approach.

Services

Our services range from high-level strategic consulting, all the way to delivering research-backed, developer hand-off ready product mockups and prototypes. We meet you wherever you're at in your lifecycle, understand your goals, and go from there.

Rates

The first hour is always on the house, with options after ranging from hourly to project-based pricing. We understand budgets are always tight, so we'll work with you to find something sustainable for everyone.
