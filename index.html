About Me

I am currently part of the UX team at Sherwin-Williams, helping design applications powering the future of their Automotive business. I was previously on the UX team at Alliance Data Card Services, where I helped design and influence the direction of their digital products & capabilities, with a focus on the credit card Account Center platform.

As a designer, my process revolves around getting out of the users way. Hearing "that was easy" during usability testing is what makes me tick. I'm grateful to have a career that allows me to positively impact the digital lives of people around the world.
