My Volunteer Adventure

Branding, Web Design, Print Design

When a high school friend asked me for help with her startup volunteer organization, I jumped at the opportunity. Over the course of three months, I worked with her on creating a unique brand identity and a beautiful website that fit her vision.

Brand Suggestions & Logo Concepts

After meeting and discussing her ideas for MVA, I began researching other volunteer organizations to get a feel for common branding practices in the industry. I put together suggested branding boards for her consideration, and created various logo and graphic concepts.

Wireframes and Mockups

Upon creating a basic hierarchy for the content my friend provided, I put together wireframes for the most important pages of the site. After a few rounds of feedback, I was ready to create high fidelity mockups of these pages.

Final Site

I decided to leverage Wordpress for this project, in part due to the vast number of plugins that would ease the development of the site's more advanced features. I ended up using a responsive theme downloaded from ThemeForest as the base of the site, with heavy customization to the theme's CSS to better match the look and feel of the mocks. Development and testing took about four weeks, and MyVolunteerAdventure.org was launched in early July 2014.
